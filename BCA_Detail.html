BCA: Course Details, Eligibility, Admission, Fees
BCA or Bachelor of Computer Application is a three year undergraduate programme which deals with computer science and its application concepts. The BCA course teaches students data structures, database systems and computer networks. The BCA course has a large scope for undergraduates as most industries require computer applications and experts for a smooth run of their business.
